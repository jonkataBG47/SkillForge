About SkillForge

SkillForge is a lightweight platform designed to help learners and educators discover, organize, and track skills, resources, and learning pathways in a clear, structured way.

Project idea

The project aims to collect and present educational resources and skills grouped by categories and learning paths. It provides a simple searchable index of skills and related resources so users can quickly find materials to learn or teach specific competencies.

What users can do

  • Search for skills by title or category.
  • Browse resource pages and learning paths curated for each skill.
  • View short descriptions and estimated effort for skills.
  • Use learning paths to plan a sequence of skills and resources.

Goal and purpose

The goal of SkillForge is to make skill discovery and learning planning simple and accessible. The platform is intended for learners who want a clear roadmap to grow their abilities, and for educators or content creators who want to share structured learning pathways and resources.
